# Launch — Phases 9-10

## Phase 9: Store Assets

### 9.1 Screenshot Strategy

Plan 5 screens telling a conversion story:

| Slide | Purpose | Pattern | Copy Formula |
|-------|---------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Hook | Hero screen + main value prop | "{Benefit} in {timeframe}" |
| 2 | Core Feature | Key functionality demo | "Easily {action} with {feature}" |
| 3 | Differentiator | What makes you unique | "Unlike {competitor}, we {diff}" |
| 4 | Social Proof | Stats, reviews, achievements | "{Number}+ {users/results}" |
| 5 | CTA | Download motivation | "Start {benefit} today" |

### 9.2 Screenshot Specs

| Platform | Size (px) | Device | Required? |
|----------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| iOS 6.7" | 1290x2796 | iPhone 15 Pro Max | Yes |
| iOS 6.5" | 1284x2778 | iPhone 14 Pro Max | Yes |
| iOS 5.5" | 1242x2208 | iPhone 8 Plus | Yes |
| iPad 12.9" | 2048x2732 | iPad Pro | If iPad app |
| Android | Min 320, max 3840 | Pixel 9 | 16:9 or 9:16 |

### 9.3 Screenshot Copy

For each of 5 slides, generate using brand voice:
- **Headline:** max 30 characters
- **Subheadline:** max 60 characters
- Must match tone from Phase 3

### 9.4 Feature Graphic (Google Play)

- **Size:** 1024x500px
- **Content:** Logo + device mockup + tagline on brand color background
- **Forbidden:** CTA buttons, award badges, pricing info

### 9.5 Screenshot Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Previewed.app | 3D device mockups | Free tier |
| Screenshots.pro | Panoramic screenshots | Free tier |
| AppLaunchpad | Multi-language export (28+ lang) | Paid |
| AppScreens | Automated screenshot generation | Paid |

**Output:** Write `store-assets.md`.

### 10.2 Content Pillars

Define 3-4 themes for consistent posting:

| Pillar | Share | Content Type | Example |
|--------|-------|-------------|---------|
| Product | 40% | Features, updates, tips | "Did you know {feature}?" |
| Value | 30% | Industry insights, data | "{Stat} about {problem}" |
| Social Proof | 20% | Reviews, milestones | "Just hit {N} users!" |
| Behind Scenes | 10% | Dev journey, building | "Week 12 of building {app}" |

### 10.3 Ad Creative Strategy

| Platform | Format | Best For |

| Google UAC | Multiple sizes (auto) | Search intent users |
| Apple Search Ads | Store screenshots | High-intent users |

### 10.4 Master Template Concept

Design ONE layout that Smart Resize adapts to all formats:
- Logo position: top-left or center
- Brand color background with gradient or texture
- Device mockup placement (left/center)
- Text area for headline (brand voice)

Use Canva "Magic Switch" or Adobe Express "Resize" for all platform sizes.
